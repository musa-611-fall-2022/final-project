Food-Near-My-Stop

Hungry on the bus? Use this app to explore food in walking distance of the bus stop you intend to get down at.

Click here for pop-up instructions!

× How to use this application:
First, select your bus stop. To find your stop, either type in the name of the stop in the area below the map, or select the direction of your bus route to find your stop on the map. Then, click on your stop's marker on the map to activate the walking radius around it. Now you can view the restaurants within your walking radius. You can hover over these food place markers to see their names and cuisines. You can also search for restaurants using this app to see if the place you want to go to is near your stop.
