What is the "I Spy Somerville" Discovery Map?

This map features a collection of all of the various buildings, objects, and features collected by the residents of Somerville, Massachusetts as part of the Somerville Historic Preservation Commission's "I Spy Somerville" Program. Click a marker on the map to learn more about the specific entry, and use the selection tools below to filter entries by their submittor, challenge category, and location in Somerville!
